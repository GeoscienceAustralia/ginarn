◆ sum

int SpaceWeatherData::sum = 0

Planetary 3-hour Range Index (Kp) (kp1 to kp8)

Sum of the 8 Kp indices for the day: Kp has values of 0o, 0+, 1-, 1o, 1+, 2-, 2o, 2+, ... , 8o, 8+, 9-, 9o, which are expressed in steps of one third unit. These values are multiplied by 10 and rounded to an integer value
